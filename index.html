About Us

Who are we?

Al Rayan Technical Services L.L.C. is the Interior Fit Out & Specialized MEP Contracting Company established in 2006. Operating over the years as fit out & specialized MEP Services provider to various prestigious clients, developers and contractors, Al Rayan has gained a wealth of experience in many sectors of the fit out and construction industry. Al Rayan continuously strives to exceed customer's needs and expectations by delivering quality interiors, within budget and on time.

We firmly believe that collaborative functioning with our clients establishes a sense of trust and transparency. Our strong desire to keep this culture going in our daily routine leads to the lasting relationships with all our clients which in turn rewards us with repeat business.

Mission

• Strive hard continuously to ensure timely delivery of Projects & Services with high quality workmanship and utmost satisfaction to Client

• To continually innovate, develop, adopt most modern methods, procedures and materials to enhance productivity and cost effectiveness to our Client

• To be a natural and most preferred choice to our clients by continuous improvement and providing cost effective solutions / projects on time

Vision

To become the market leader in Interior Fit Out and Electromechanical Industry that is renowned for Ethics, Commitments, Excellence, Performance, Quality and Reliability

Values

• Focus on Quality and Innovation

• Honour our commitments

• Conduct our business in responsible, ethical and sustainable way

Services

What we Do?

Design & Build

We provide comprehensive range of services to cater to various robust requirement of our Client, right from interior concept design to completion of the project, turnkey requirements, detailed design & shop drawings preparation, MEP designs for various applications, value engineering solutions, complete execution of the project till handing over and Authority approval etc. We are renowned for having successfully handled several prestigious jobs with immovable dead line.

Fit Outs

We are specialized in turnkey interior fit outs of offices, banks, Retails, supermarkets, high end restaurants etc. Our team is experienced over a decade of carrying out the work with highest quality & workmanship standard with on time delivery/completion.

MEP Services

We are motivated by the concept of “making building come alive” which is the driving force for us to excel in Design and execution of top quality MEP projects. Our MEP team is fully qualified & well experienced in handling MEP Projects which includes Airconditioning, Extract & Ventilation systems, Wet services such as plumbing & drainage, Fire Fighting systems, Lighting & Power, Fire alarm & emergency lighting system, ELV systems and telephone & Data network cabling/system etc. We play a very constructive role in terms of offering value engineering and help our Clients delivering projects within the budget and timeline.

Building Maintenance Services

We have dedicated team who undertake annual maintenance service contract of the Buildings & MEP systems. We are specialized in energy audit & energy conservation methods/procedures of building MEP systems and carry out these services to assist our client to save their utility bill substantially.
